Who Am I

Well it's always difficult for me to start, but once I am into anything I try my best to get the best out of any opportunity. I believe in action more than words. I respect each and every individual around me and value their presence.

I am fun loving, talkative person, born on 19th July in Pune. I am proud to be son of Arch. Ashok Zamwar and Jayshree Zamwar and I adore them to bring me in this beautiful world. For me my parents and family are the foremost and my good friends are always part of my family.

I am a Computer Science Graduate and computers has always been my field of interest. I deeply support FOSS and I am a die hard fan of Ubuntu Systems. And that is because of it's simplicity and ease of use. I always believe in being simple. "Simple Living, high thinking", though I am unsure how many have achieved this infinitely developing thinking heights in the technological era, where each new day dawns with new technologies and inventions. I have worked in Tokyo for approximately one and a half year and have learnt some valuable things in Life. Some of them are Value of Time, Efficient planning and most important are being Humble and Respectful.

In my free time you will find me trying to learn new developments in the field of computers, blogging about some new interesting stuff I come accross or may be watching some Television Series like Two and a half Men, The Big Bang Theory, Castle. I am also fond of Computer Games like Counter Strike and FIFA. My favourite movies are Die Hard, Matrix, Shawshank Redemption, Finding Nemo, If Only, Serendipidity and the list is never ending. I love to watch F.R.I.E.N.D.S. - the very popular English Series, and I watch it to get relaxed whenever I am tired or tensed. Also, I like to read some Novels and magazines which have a very good review and are worth reading. Some which I have already read are "Wings of Fire" by APJ Kalam, "Rich Dad Poor Dad", "Linux for you", "Five point Someone".
